Short Biography

I am currently pursuing the M.S. degree at Graduate Institute of Networking and Multimedia, National Taiwan University, Taipei City, Taiwan. I am now a member of NTU imLab, and advised by Prof. Yi-Ping Hung.

I received the B.S. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) from Tamkang University, New Taipei City, Taiwan, in 2023. From 2020 to 2023, I joined the Advanced Mixed-Operation System Laboratory (AMOS Lab.) of Tamkang University, and conducted research related to Computer Vision (CV) under the supervision of Prof. Jen-Shiun Chiang.

I am very active in international academic activities and have published several conference papers and journal papers in IEEE and Springer Nature. And I am IEEE Student Member and IET Student Member. I'm serving as the Program Committee member (PC member) of The Pacific Rim International Conference on Artificial Intelligence (PRICAI) 2023.

Research Interest

I’m interested in Deep Learning (DL) and Computer Vision (CV). My current research focuses include:

  • Detection of Bone Fractures on X-ray Images: (Medical Image Computing, Fracture Detection, YOLO, X-ray Imaging)
  • Image Restoration Based on Low Computing Platform: (Transformer, Single Image Super-Resolution, Raspberry Pi)
  • Binarization of Degenerate Documents: (Discrete Wavelet Transform, Generative Adversarial Network, Semantic Segmentation)
  • Image Recognition Based on Low Computing Platform: (Convolutional Neural Network, YOLO, Image Classification, Object Detection, Raspberry Pi)
