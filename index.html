Short Bio

I'm currently (2019-present) pursuing the degree with the Division of Electronics and Information Engineering, Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Tamkang University, Taiwan. Since 2020, he has been conducting research with Professor Jen-Shiun Chiang with the Advanced Mixed-Operation System Laboratory (AMOS Lab.), Tamkang University.

I’m interested in Machine Learning (ML), Computer Vision (CV), and their applications in Embedded Systems. My current research focuses include:

  • Detection of Bone Fractures on X-ray Images: (Medical Image Computing, Fracture detection, YOLO, X-ray Imaging)
  • Image Restoration Based on Low Computing Platform: (Transformer, Single Image Super-Resolution, Raspberry Pi)
  • Binarization of Degenerate Documents: (Discrete Wavelet Transform, Generative Adversarial Network, Semantic Segmentation)
  • Image Recognition Based on Low Computing Platform: (Convolutional Neural Network, YOLO, Image Classification, Object Detection, Raspberry Pi)
