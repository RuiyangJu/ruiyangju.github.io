Short Bio

I'm currently (2019-present) pursuing the degree with the Division of Electronics and Information Engineering, Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Tamkang University, Taiwan. Since 2020, he has been conducting research with Professor Jen-Shiun Chiang with the Prospective Mixed Operation System Laboratory, Tamkang University.

I’m interested in Machine Learning, Computer vision, and their applications in Embedded Systems. My current research focuses include:

  • Image Recognition Based on Edge Computing Platform: (Model Compression, Convolutional Neural Network, Image Classification, Object Detection)
  • Binarization of Degenerate Documents: (Discrete Wavelet Transform, Generative Adversarial Network, Seman‑ tic Segmentation)
  • Image Super‑Resolution Based on Edge Computing Platform: (Transformer, Super‑Resolution, Raspberry Pi)
