Short Biography

I am currently pursuing the M.S. degree at Graduate Institute of Networking and Multimedia, National Taiwan University, Taipei City, Taiwan. I am now a member of NTU imLab, and advised by Prof. Yi-Ping Hung. I received the B.S. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) from Tamkang University, New Taipei City, Taiwan, in 2023. From 2020 to 2023, I joined the Advanced Mixed-Operation System Laboratory (AMOS Lab.) of Tamkang University, and conducted research related to Computer Vision (CV) under the supervision of Prof. Jen-Shiun Chiang. I am very active in international academic activities and have published several conference papers and journal papers as the first author. I am IET Member (MIET) and IEEE Graduate Student Member. I’m serving as the reviewer of KBS, IJMLC, etc., and in the program committees of PRICAI 2023, 2024.

Research Interest

My primary research areas include Deep Learning (DL), Neural Networks (NN), Computer Vision (CV), and Image Processing (IP). In addition, I have contributed to some projects in other fields, such as Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Speech & Audio. My completed and current research focuses on the following topics:

  • 3D Facial Expression Reconstruction: Computer Graphics, 3D Computer Vision, 3D Morphable Model, 3D Face Reconstruction, 3D Face Animation
  • Color Degraded Document Image Binarization: Image Generation, Image Ehancement, Image Binarization, Generative Adversarial Network, Discrete Wavelet Transform
  • Fracture Detection on Pediatric Wrist Trauma X-ray Images: Medical Image Processing, Medical Imaging Diagnostics, Object Detection, Fracture Detection, You Only Look Once, Attention Mechanism
  • Layer and Block Connection Strategy: Image Recognition, Image Classification, Image Super-Resolution, Convolutional Neural Network, Transformer, Network Architecture Design, Residual Learning
